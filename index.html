Developed by Urbancorp, the Westside Gallery Lofts at 150 Sudbury St is a study in contrasts. The kitchens are high-gloss Italian made Scavolini brand, but the rest of the finishes such as the walls, the floors and ceilings are about as raw industrial concrete as you will ever see anywhere. The location is excellent, situated between King St and Queen St with south views overlooking Liberty Village.
